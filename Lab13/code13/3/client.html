GATE Application

GATE examination is taken by candidates in India to pursue post-graduation in Engineering fields like Electronics, Computer Sceince, Electrical, Mechanical, Civil, etc. The exam takes place typically around 1st or 2nd week of February and results are announced 1 month later. The exam really important for those who want get admission in IIT's and IISC. The syllabus of any stream covers the basics undergraduate courses. The questions are more of a analytical and conceptual type rather than recall type.
